    <h3 id="cost-pentagon-slush-fund-about-header">About This Counter: Cost of Pentagon Slush Fund</h3>
    <p>This counter displays the estimated cost, since 2001, of diverting war funds from the Overseas Contingency Operations (OCO) account to pad the regular Pentagon budget, in nominal (non-inflation-adjusted) dollars. This applies to funding that has been appropriated by Congress for the OCO account through the end of fiscal year 2015 on Sept. 30, 2015.
