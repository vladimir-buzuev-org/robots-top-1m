  <h3>LCA2018</h3>
  <p>Being held at the University of Technology, Sydney linux.conf.au is the largest linux and open source conference in the Asia-Pacific region. Run annually since 1999, it brings together the Australian, New Zealand and international community for standout presentations, demonstrations and relationship building.</p>
  <p>It is a deeply technical conference and pre-emininent practitioners in the field, both professionals and hobbyists, are expected to attend. linux.conf.au attracts between 500 to 800 delegates annually.</p>
  <p>Still unsure what linux.conf.au is all about? You can take a first hand look at it through videos from past linux.conf.au on YouTube: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/linuxconfau2017/videos">LCA2017</a> or <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/LinuxAustralia/channels">earlier</a>.</p>
