<h1>PicoSearch web service ended on July 1, 2014<br>
</h1>
<p>After 17 years of providing site search, we are informing you that PicoSearch has ended. Existing PicoSearch customers were sent email notification and contact information at least three months prior to closure. Refunds for paid accounts with expiration dates past July 1 were processed after the closure date.  Inquiries can be made to <a href="mailto:support@picosearch.com">support@picosearch.com</a>.</p>
<p>There are replacement options for site search available on the internet. We regret to cause you this inconvenience and appreciate your patronage over the years. We wish you all the best with your internet ventures.<br>
</p>
