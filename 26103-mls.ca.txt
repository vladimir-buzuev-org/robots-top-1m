<div class="at-above-post-page" data-url="http://www.crea.ca/mls-realtor/" data-title="Were you looking for REALTOR.ca?"></div><p><a href="https://www.realtor.ca/"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-18803" src="http://d18hrjrodoay6x.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/banner_realtor_en.jpg" alt="" width="888" height="411" srcset="http://d18hrjrodoay6x.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/banner_realtor_en.jpg 888w, http://d18hrjrodoay6x.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/banner_realtor_en-768x355.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 888px) 100vw, 888px" /></a></p>
<h3>Why have we brought you here?</h3>
<p>To help Canadians better understand the difference between MLS® Systems and <a href="https://www.realtor.ca/">REALTOR.ca</a> we felt it was important to share the following:</p>
<h3>1. What exactly is an MLS® System?</h3>
<p>MLS® Systems are cooperative selling systems operated by real estate Boards and Associations in Canada. They are accessible to REALTOR® Members of those Boards and Associations who have agreed to represent your interests and share remuneration from the transaction with a cooperating REALTOR® Member. MLS® Systems contain detailed information and numerous search tools, all designed to match people with the properties that fit their exact requirements.</p>
<h3>2. So how is REALTOR.ca different?</h3>
<p><a href="http://REALTOR.ca">REALTOR.ca</a> is Canada&#8217;s most popular and trusted real estate platform displaying residential and commercial listings from REALTORS® across the country. It is important to note that REALTOR.ca is not an MLS® System. It is an advertising website that The Canadian Real Estate Association (CREA) created to give REALTORS® across Canada greater listing exposure to a national and international audience.</p>
<p>The listing content advertised on REALTOR.ca comes from the various MLS® Systems operated by Real Estate Boards and Associations across Canada. REALTOR.ca is paid for by REALTORS® and is owned and operated in their interest by CREA.</p>
<p><strong>In summary:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The old MLS.ca address (URL) was replaced with <a href="https://www.realtor.ca/">REALTOR.ca</a>.</li>
<li>If you are looking for property listings and information on REALTORS® from across Canada – visit <a href="https://www.realtor.ca/">REALTOR.ca</a>.</li>
<li>If you have bookmarks for URLs that include MLS.ca, please update them to <a href="https://www.realtor.ca/">REALTOR.ca</a> to ensure that they still work.</li>
</ul>
