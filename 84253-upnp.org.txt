<section class="what-upnp">
	<h2>WHAT IS OCF?</h2>
	<div class="wrap">
    	<p>The Open Connectivity Foundation is dedicated to ensuring secure interoperability for consumers, businesses and industries by delivering a standard communications platform, a bridging specification,  <a href="https://openconnectivity.org/developer/iotivity">an open source implementation</a> and a <a href="https://openconnectivity.org/certification">certification program</a> allowing devices to communicate regardless of form factor, operating system, service provider, transport technology or ecosystem.</p>
<p>The <a href="https://openconnectivity.org/about/membership-list">industry leading companies</a> involved in OCF believe that secure and reliable device discovery and connectivity is a foundational component to enable IoT. The good news is that it is underway.</p>
<a href="https://openconnectivity.org/foundation" class="button">Learn More</a>
    </div>
</section>
