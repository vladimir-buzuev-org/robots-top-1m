      <p>Hello,</p>

<p>We launched Ta-da List back in January of 2005 so everyone could have a fast and free to-do list app in their web browser. There wasn’t much out there that was good in 2005.</p>

<p>My, how things have changed. Now there are dozens and dozens of great free to-do list apps on the web and dozens more on the iPhone, Android, and Windows Phone.</p>

<p>Because we haven’t given Ta-da List much attention in the last few years, and as part of refocusing our efforts on <a href="/">Basecamp</a> — we’ve decided to retire Ta-da List. Ta-da List will no longer be signing up new customers.</p>

<p>But don’t worry — any to-do lists you already created here at TadaList.com will continue to work. Just <a href="https://123.tadalist.com/session/new">sign in</a> to access your lists.</p>

<p>Thanks to everyone who used Ta-da List over the years.</p>

<p>Onwards,</p>
