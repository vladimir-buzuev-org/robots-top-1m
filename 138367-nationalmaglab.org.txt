<h4>Research Initiatives</h4>
</div>
<div class="contentwrapper">
<div class="leftcol"><a href="/research/research-initiatives/materials"><img src="/images/layout/mainmaglab/homepage_materials.png" alt="Research Initiatives - Materials" title="Research Initiatives - Materials" /></a>
<h3 class="blueheading"><a href="/research/research-initiatives/materials">MATERIALS</a></h3>
<p>Scientists use our magnets to explore semiconductors, superconductors, newly-grown crystals, buckyballs and materials from the natural world  research that reveals the secret workings of materials and empowers us to develop new technologies.</p>
<p><a href="/research/research-initiatives/materials" class="bluelinelink">Read more </a></p>
</div>
<div class="middlecol"><a href="/research/research-initiatives/energy"><img src="/images/layout/mainmaglab/homepage_energy.png" alt="Research Initiatives - Energy" title="Research Initiatives - Energy" /></a>
<h3 class="blueheading"><a href="/research/research-initiatives/energy">ENERGY</a></h3>
<p>Scientists here are working to optimize petroleum refining, advance potential bio-fuels such as pine needles and algae, and fundamentally change the way we store and deliver energy by developing better batteries.</p>
<p><a href="/research/research-initiatives/energy" class="bluelinelink">Read more </a></p>
</div>
<div class="rightcol"><a href="/research/research-initiatives/life"><img src="/images/layout/mainmaglab/homepage_life.png" alt="Research Initiatives - Life" title="Research Initiatives - Life" /></a>
<h3 class="blueheading"><a href="/research/research-initiatives/life">LIFE</a></h3>
<p>With the worlds strongest MRI magnet, scientists here study everything from living animals to individual cells, from proteins to disease-fighting molecules found in plants and animals  work that could improve treatment of AIDS, cancer, Alzheimers and other diseases.</p>
<p><a href="/research/research-initiatives/life" class="bluelinelink">Read more </a></p>

<h4>About the Lab</h4>
</div>
<img src="/images/layout/mainmaglab/Map_withlocations.jpg" alt="Map of MagLab locations and institutions." class="pull-center" title="Map of MagLab locations and institutions." />
<div class="sideboxwrapper-no-ul">
<p>The <strong>National High Magnetic Field Laboratory</strong> (MagLab) is the largest and highest powered magnet lab in the world. With facilities at <strong>Florida State University</strong>, the <strong>University of Florida</strong> and <strong>Los Alamos National Laboratory</strong> in New Mexico, the MagLab offers scientists from across the globe <strong>free access</strong> to unique instruments and expertise, advancing basic science, engineering and technology in the 21st century.</p>
