<h1 class="large">Welcome to Bookstart&nbsp;</h1>
<p class="large">Sharing books with your child early can help create a lifelong reader. Bookstart is here to guide you on your child’s reading journey from 0-5 years.</p>
<p>Every child in England is entitled to a Bookstart pack at 0-12 months and 3-4 years. Find out how you can get your free packs and explore our website. You'll find amazing pages for all the family, including interactive storybooks, booklists, bookfinders, activities and much more. Happy reading, singing and rhyming.</p></div></div>
