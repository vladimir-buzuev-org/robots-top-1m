			<div class="article-header sc normal" data-role="header"><div class="article-title-wrap"><h2 class="article-title entry-title" itemprop="headline"><a href="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/2015/07/gianna-sabrina-turn-two/" title="Permalink to Gianna &#038; Sabrina Turn Two!!" rel="bookmark" itemprop="url">Gianna &#038; Sabrina Turn Two!!</a></h2></div><div class="article-meta article-meta-top"><span class="article-date article-meta-item">

				<p>Over the years I have been lucky enough to photograph hundreds of new little cuties and I cherish each and every one. A few times a year I get to experience a &#8220;first&#8221; along with these little ones during their first photo shoot. This session was my first time photographing a baby that was yet to be named! I absolutely love the naming process and everything that goes into it for each and every family. We realize how our kiddos will carry this name through their lives and it will be such a huge part of their identity. This sweet 5 day old baby girl was born at home with her adoring family who have yet to decide on this very very important part of her story. I am still on pins and needles waiting to find out and will update the post when I do! Here are a few favorite images from our session&#8230;it was so hard to choose!</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">I have such a soft spot for a newborn baby in a hanging scale or laying on a scale, it is so amazing how little they start out. 			<div class="article-header sc normal" data-role="header"><div class="article-title-wrap"><h2 class="article-title entry-title" itemprop="headline"><a href="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/2015/05/mckinley-may/" title="Permalink to McKinley May" rel="bookmark" itemprop="url">McKinley May</a></h2></div><div class="article-meta article-meta-top"><span class="article-date article-meta-item">
				<time class="updated" datetime="2015-05-22" itemprop="datePublished">Friday, May 22, 2015 </time>
			</span></div></div>
				<div class="article-content sc pp-img-protect-replace" data-role="content" itemprop="mainContentOfPage">

				<p>I know, I have been super bad about the blog but I just couldn&#8217;t keep McKinley May all to myself, I had to share! My good friends and neighbors were sweet enough to let me get to know and photograph their baby daughter this past week. I had so much fun trying out a few new props and concepts I have been working on. Here are the results&#8230;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
		<span class='pp-img-protect pp-img-protect-aligncenter' style='width:900px;'>
			<img class='pp-overlay' style='width:900px;height:600px;' src='http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif' alt='' /><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" data-lazyload-src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Newborn-Baby-In-Army-Helmet.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2849 ov-done" width="900" height="600" alt="Newborn Baby In Army Helmet" /></span><span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:900px;height:600px;"><a href="" class="pp-pinit-link"><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40" height="20" alt="" /></a></span></span>I thought this shot was especially fitting for this Memorial Day weekend. McKinley&#8217;s Daddy brought out his helmet and boots for his new little lady. Who doesn&#8217;t love a sweet soft newborn baby with Daddy&#8217;s military gear&#8230;love it!<span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
		<span class='pp-img-protect pp-img-protect-aligncenter' style='width:900px;'>
			<img class='pp-overlay' style='width:900px;height:600px;' src='http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif' alt='' /><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" data-lazyload-src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Newborn-Baby-In-Gerber-Crate.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2850 ov-done" width="900" height="600" alt="Newborn Baby In Gerber Crate" /></span><span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:900px;height:600px;"><a href="" class="pp-pinit-link"><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40" height="20" alt="" /></a></span></span>This Gerber crate is a newly acquired prop from the Rose Bowl Swap meet. This little girl is definitely a &#8220;Gerber Baby&#8221;, so adorable!<span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
		<span class='pp-img-protect pp-img-protect-aligncenter' style='width:900px;'>
			<img class='pp-overlay' style='width:900px;height:600px;' src='http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif' alt='' /><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" data-lazyload-src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Newborn-Baby-On-Iron-Doll-Bed.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2851 ov-done" width="900" height="600" alt="Newborn Baby On Iron Doll Bed" /></span><span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:900px;height:600px;"><a href="" class="pp-pinit-link"><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40" height="20" alt="" /></a></span></span>I usually photograph this bed with the princess and the pea blankets which I love but this more simplified version was really beautiful!<span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
		<span class='pp-img-protect pp-img-protect-aligncenter' style='width:900px;'>
			<img class='pp-overlay' style='width:900px;height:889px;' src='http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif' alt='' /><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" data-lazyload-src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Newborn-Baby-On-Swing.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2852 ov-done" width="900" height="889" alt="Newborn Baby On Swing" /></span><span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:900px;height:889px;"><a href="" class="pp-pinit-link"><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40" height="20" alt="" /></a></span></span>Ok so drumroll&#8230;I have been drilling holes, staining wood, and clamping rope for a couple of weeks now and have made 3 of these swings. This was my first test and I love the result. McKinley was the perfect model and didn&#8217;t budge up there, she was wonderful! I even got to put a new material behind her&#8230;love my dandelions!<span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
		<span class='pp-img-protect pp-img-protect-aligncenter' style='width:900px;'>
			<img class='pp-overlay' style='width:900px;height:900px;' src='http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif' alt='' /><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/blank.gif" data-lazyload-src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Newborn-Baby-Posing.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2853 ov-done" width="900" height="900" alt="Newborn Baby Posing" /></span><span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:900px;height:900px;"><a href="" class="pp-pinit-link"><img src="http://www.dianahenderson.com/photography/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40" height="20" alt="" /></a></span></span>After all of the fun sets and backdrops, it was nice to finish it off with some  simple, timeless, and gorgeous shots of McKinley in all her sweetness. Who doesn&#8217;t love these poses: the tushie, taco, side and the beloved frog pose&#8221;!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Thank you again for sharing your gorgeous little lady with me! Love you guys! XOXO</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Props Used: Burlap Backdrop, Grey Wood Floordrop, Gerber Crate, Blue Stripe Burlap, White Wood Floordrop, Pink Iron Bed, White Ruffle Backdrop, Pink Dandi Backdrop, Gray Swing</p>
