<p>InBIA is a global non profit that represents over 2,200 entrepreneurship support organizations. We are the largest member-based entrepreneurial support network in the world. For over 30 years, InBIA has remained the leader in providing the best industry resources for building thriving entrepreneurial ecosystems through sustainable entrepreneurial programs across a wide scope of industries and disciplines.
