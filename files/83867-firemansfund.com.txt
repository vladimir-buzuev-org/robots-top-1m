        <h1>Fireman’s Fund Insurance Company Information</h1>
        
			</div>
      <div class="content-body">
			<div class="cms-page"><div>
	<p>Claims, support and contact information for Fireman’s Fund policyholders and brokers.</p>
<p><strong>Fireman’s Fund Insurance Company is now integrated into Allianz Global Corporate &amp; Specialty (AGCS). This consolidation means that current Fireman’s Fund policyholders and their brokers will benefit from the same focused insurance offerings, but with the added bonus of Allianz’s global reach and infrastructure.</strong></p>
<p>Support and claim centers remain open to answer your questions and process claims in the rapid manner that Fireman’s Fund prided itself on for more than 150 years. Please view the contact and claims information below for all of your insurance needs.</p>
