<div id="c3788" class="csc-default"><p>EMOTA’s European eCommerce Trustmark allows consumers to find reliable and trustworthy merchants selling online. Websites with the EMOTA Trustmark comply with Europe’s best trading practices on the market.</p>
<p>EMOTA’s Trustmark are awarded in conjunction with the national Trustmark and by the issuer of the national Trustmark.&nbsp; In a way, the EMOTA Trustmark guarantees that the national Trustmark complies with Europe’s best trading practices. </p>
<p>Cross-border shopper in particular who see the EMOTA Trustmark next to the national Trustmark can feel assured that European best trading practices are applied.</p></div>
