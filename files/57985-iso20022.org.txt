<h1>ISO 20022 message dashboard</h1>

<p>The ISO 20022 message dashboard gives an overall picture of the five financial business domains in scope of ISO 20022. Its purpose is to show which business processes are already supported either by existing ISO 20022 message definitions or by candidate message definitions covered by an approved Business Justification.</p>

<p> </p>

<div class="gui-teaser gui-teaser-world">
<div>

			</td>

			<p><a class="gui-media-more" href="https://www.iso20022.org/securities_dashboard.page">Read more</a></p>
			</td>
			<td>
			<h3>Trade Services</h3>

			<p>Messages supporting procurement, trade finance products and services, forecasting, reconciliation, accounting, remittance information, etc.</p>

			<p><a class="gui-media-more" href="https://www.iso20022.org/trade_services_dashboard.page">Read more</a></p>
