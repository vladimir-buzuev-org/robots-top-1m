<h2>You have cookies turned off</h2>
<p>To use this website, cookies must be enabled in your browser. To enable cookies, follow the instructions for your browser below.</p>
</div>
<div class="facebook">
<p><strong>Facebook App: Open links in External Browser</strong></p>
<div>There is a specific issue with the Facebook in-app browser intermittently making requests to websites without cookies that had previously been set. This appears to be a defect in the browser which should be addressed soon. The simplest approach to avoid this problem is to continue to use the Facebook app but not use the in-app browser. This can be done through the following steps:</div>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Open the settings menu by clicking the hamburger menu in the top right</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Choose &#8220;App Settings&#8221; from the menu</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Turn on the option &#8220;Links Open Externally&#8221; (This will use the device&#8217;s default browser)</p>
</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="windows">
<h2>Windows</h2>
<div class="ie">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Internet Explorer 9</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Open the Internet <a id="_GoBack" name="_GoBack"></a>Browser</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click Tools (or &#8220;gear&#8221; icon at top right hand corner) &gt; Internet Options &gt; Privacy &gt; Advanced</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Check Override automatic cookie handling</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For First-party Cookies and Third-party Cookies click Accept</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click OK and OK</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div><strong>Enabling Cookies in Internet Explorer 10, 11</strong></div>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Open the Internet <a id="_GoBack" name="_GoBack"></a>Browser</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the Tools button, and then click Internet Options.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the Privacy tab, and then, under Settings, move the slider to the bottom to allow all cookies, and then click OK.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click OK</p>
</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="firefox">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Firefox</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Open the Firefox browser</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click Tools &gt; Options &gt; Privacy &lt; Use custom settings for history</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Check Accept cookies from sites</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Check Accept third party cookies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select Keep until: they expire</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click OK</p>
</li>
</ol>
</div>
<div class="chrome">
<p><strong>Enabling Cookies in Google Chrome</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>
