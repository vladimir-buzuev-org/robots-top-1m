      <div class="region region-content">
    <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system">

    
  <div class="content">
      <div class="grid grid-spaced">
    <div class="col"><p>Our aim is to ensure that people with learning disabilities continue to get better lives.We do this by gaining knowledge, improving practice and influencing of policy through our work directly with people with learning disabilities.</p>
<p class="intro">We believe that people with learning disabilities should have the same rights and the same choices as everyone else in society.</p>
