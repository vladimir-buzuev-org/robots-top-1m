<div><span style="font-size: 14pt;font-weight:bold;font-color: #cc0000"> <h2 align="center"><span style="color: #ee2e24">National CASA is Transforming Lives</span></h2></div>

<div><p align="center"><span style="font-size: 10pt;font-weight: normal; font-color: #333333"> <div style="margin:10px 50px 5px 50px;">

Nearly 700,000 children experience abuse or neglect each year. Instead of playing with neighbors and making happy
family memories, they&#8217;re attending court hearings, adjusting to new foster homes and transitioning to new schools. That&#8217;s
a heavy burden for a child to carry. With a Court Appointed Special Advocate (CASA) or guardian ad litem (GAL)
volunteer dedicated to their case, America&#8217;s most vulnerable children will have someone speaking up for their best
interests. With your support, more children will have the opportunity to thrive in a safe and loving home.
</p></div>
