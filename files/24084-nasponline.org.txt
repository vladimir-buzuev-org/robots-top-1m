<h3 class="title-2 ">
    About NASP
</h3>

<div class="margin-large formatted ">
	<p>The National Association of School Psychologists (NASP) represents and supports the profession of school psychology by advancing effective practices to improve students' learning, behavior and mental health, and by maintaining essential standards for ethics and practice. <a href="/utility/about-nasp">Read more</a></p>

<div class="content-box margin-large  normal">
		<h3 class="content-box-title">The NASP Practice Model</h3>
    
<div class="margin-large formatted ">
	<p><img alt="" class="img-right" src="Images/content-practice-model.jpg">The NASP <em>Model for Comprehensive and Integrated School Psychological Services</em>, also known as the NASP Practice Model, represents NASP's official policy regarding the delivery of school psychological services. NASP has developed <a href="/standards-and-certification/nasp-practice-model/nasp-practice-model-implementation-guide">this implementation guide</a> as a resource to help school psychologists through the process of advocating for and implementing the model in their school or district.</p>
