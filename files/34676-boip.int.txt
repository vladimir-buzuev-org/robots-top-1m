<p class="title">BOIP</p>

<blockquote>
<p>The Benelux Office for Intellectual Property (BOIP) is the official body for the registration of trademarks and designs in the Benelux.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>In addition, the BOIP offers the possibility to formally record the existence of ideas, concepts, designs prototypes and the like in an i-DEPOT.</p>

<p class="title">What is Intellectual Property?</p>

<p>Intellectual property is the collective term for rights to intellectual creations such as books, music, trademarks, designs, inventions, software, texts and photographs. A single creation may be protected by multiple rights at the same time. The best-known intellectual property rights are trademark rights, copyrights and patent rights.</p>
