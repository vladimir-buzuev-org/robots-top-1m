<h1>Ireland is one of the best places in the world to do business</h1>

<p>Your business has found its new international home. Join over 1,200 companies, from global giants to the hottest high-growth brands, who have already chosen Ireland as their strategic European base.</p>
<a class="play" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lGw003U2HO8" target="_blank"></a></div><div class="component-share white" data-addthis-title="Ireland is one of the best places in the world to do business" data-addthis-url="http://www.idaireland.com/#no1"></div></div></div></section><div class="content-box"><a class="anchor" name="comp00005362692c00000001a04cba"><span></span></a><a name="irelandattheheartofthings"></a><section class="text-block"><div class="row"><div class="small-18 large-16 small-centered columns"><img alt="" height="406" src="/_internal/cimg!0/gpfryqlyx26ux3grd57bdgxkh8o008m" title="" width="400" />
<h2>Ireland at the heart of Europe</h2>

<p>Ireland is a committed member of the European Union and provides companies with guaranteed access to the European market.  Ireland is the only English speaking country in the Eurozone and provides an ideal hub for organisations seeking a European base. The brightest talent from across Europe is attracted here, mixing with our own to offer a multinational and multilingual melting pot of skills with a positive attitude to match.</p>
<a class="button gradient" href="/invest-in-ireland/why-invest/">Reasons to Invest</a><div class="component-share grey" data-addthis-title="Ireland at the heart of Europe" data-addthis-url="http://www.idaireland.com/#irelandattheheartofthings"></div></div></div></section><a class="anchor" name="comp00005362692c000000019e4cba"><span></span></a><section class="text-block text-center icons-grouped grey"><div class="row"><div class="small-18 large-16 small-centered columns">
<h2>Our people and your business,  a winning combination</h2>

<p>There are many reasons why companies locate themselves in Ireland, but it all starts with positive leadership and policies. Successive governments have developed an <a href="/invest-in-ireland/ireland-economy/" target="_self">open economy</a> and invested heavily to develop Ireland's infrastructure. This has allowed the IDA, a semi-state body, to continue to <a href="/how-we-help/" target="_self">attract high-performing industries</a> to our shores, even through challenging times.</p>
</div></div></section><a class="anchor" name="comp00005362692c000000019f4cba"><span></span></a><a name="probusiness"></a><section class="banner lazy" data-src="/_internal/cimg!0/o2at7gq3gi4dgmkr1e6soptqszr7po1" data-src-medium="/_internal/cimg!0/l9sp4nksynykp6jpam7pwmhh122q2bw" data-src-small="/_internal/cimg!0/ep1k4e4ioo37p6wcojgoa7lkhzuif3o"><div class="row"><div class="small-18 large-16 small-centered large-centered columns"><div class="row"><div class="large-10 columns"><div class="cell">
<h2>Pro-business</h2>

<p>We favour green lights over red tape, which is why we are one of the best countries in the world for ease of doing business (Forbes). New business is welcomed and supported by the flow of talent coming from our schools, universities and abroad, to work for high-performing companies across a range of cutting-edge sectors.</p>
<a class="button" href="/invest-in-ireland/">Invest in Ireland</a></div></div></div><div class="component-share white" data-addthis-title="Pro-business" data-addthis-url="http://www.idaireland.com/#probusiness"></div></div></div></section><a class="anchor" name="comp00005362692c00000001a24cba"><span></span></a><a name="howwehelp"></a><section class="text-block text-center icons-grouped"><div class="row"><div class="small-18 large-16 small-centered columns">
<h2>How we help</h2>

<p>IDA Ireland actively plans for success.  As Ireland's inward promotion agency, we partner with established companies, both large and small, working with them every step of the way to achieve a smooth, fast and successful set-up of their operations in Ireland.</p>
<a class="button gradient" href="/how-we-help/">How can we help you</a><div class="component-share grey" data-addthis-title="How we help" data-addthis-url="http://www.idaireland.com/#howwehelp"></div></div></div></section><a class="anchor" name="comp00005362692c00000001a14cba"><span></span></a><a name="trackrecord"></a><section class="banner lazy" data-src="/_internal/cimg!0/1c6m2opng2hz4ttjs7ya40wp8kevn0j" data-src-medium="/_internal/cimg!0/sj3ov6uv09p040zyxkb7aian3anz0td" data-src-small="/_internal/cimg!0/1irk0f36byy9kays2547vtdfiiz4xyd"><div class="row"><div class="small-18 large-16 small-centered large-centered columns"><div class="row"><div class="large-10 columns"><div class="cell">
<h2>Track record</h2>

<p>Ireland has an impeccable track record for attracting Foreign Direct Investment for over 50 years, helping Ireland's economy beat global trends. IDA's track record speaks for itself, as our partnership approach has led to increased investment, expansion and diversification of client activities.</p>
