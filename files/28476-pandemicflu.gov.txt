							<div class="row "><div class="span19 "><div class=" "><h3></h3><div><div class=" module"><div><p>An influenza pandemic is a global outbreak of a new influenza A virus. Pandemics happen when new (novel) influenza A viruses emerge which are able to infect people easily and spread from person to person in an efficient and sustained way. The United States is NOT currently experiencing an influenza pandemic.</p></div></div>
