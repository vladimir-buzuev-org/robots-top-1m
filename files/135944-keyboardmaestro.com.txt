<h2>What&rsquo;s New in Keyboard Maestro 7?</h2>

<div class="Entries">

<div class="Entry">
<h3>More Macro Triggers</h3>
<p>Trigger your macros with hot keys that are down, pressed, released, or tapped multiple times. Or trigger with a change in window focus or folder or clipboard.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Insert Action by Name</h3>
<p>Press a hot key, and then type in part of the name of an action to see a list of possibilities. Press Return or double click to insert it. With so many available actions this can save a lot of time.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Share with a click</h3>
<p>The new Share button on the toolbar lets you easily share macros to the Forum, or via messages, email and more.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Group Actions</h3>
<p>Group actions together then try, enable or disable them all. Give the group a name with the new rename feature in the gear menu so you can easily keep track of what you&rsquo;re doing.</p>
</div>


<div class="clear">
</div>


<div class="Entry">
<h3>Gear Menu </h3>
<p>Each action now includes a Gear menu to enable, disable, try, rename, add notes, set colors, or get help, as well as copying the action as text or an image for easier sharing.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Macro Inspector</h3>
<p>The Macro Inspector window tells you when you created, modified or last used a macro. You can also see how many times you&rsquo;ve used it and how much time it has saved you.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Mouse Display Window</h3>
<p>The new Mouse Display window shows the mouse coordinates as your cursor moves around the screen. Set the corner of screen or window to measure from, then copy the results into your action.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Display Text Large</h3>
<p>Now actions that return results or display text can Display Large. This is very handy for showing important results or for getting serious attention.</p>
</div>


<div class="clear">
</div>


<div class="Entry">
<h3>Integrated Help</h3>
<p>Each trigger, action, token, function, condition and collection links directly to the wiki. Option select from the addition or insertion menus to go directly to help for exactly that item. Control click on an action, or use the gear icon to call up the help you need. </p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Autocomplete</h3>
<p>As you type text tokens, functions, variables and search tokens possible completions appear right at the cursor. Select the one you need and have it entered for you.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Themed Palette styles</h3>
<p>Fancy a palette with a blue background, autumn, red wine or custom colors? With a dozen new presets, the ability to set how many columns and how they sort, your Keyboard Maestro palettes will always look just the way you want them.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>So Much More</h3>
<p>With over a hundred new features or improvements, you&rsquo;re sure to find exciting new possibilities for your work.</p>
</div>

<div class="clear">
</div>

</div>

<h2>What Else Can Keyboard Maestro Do?</h2>

<div class="Entries">

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Launch Applications</h3>
<p>Launch any application at the touch of a key. Show applications, hide them, bring them to the front, all at your command.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Text Expansion</h3>
<p>Insert any kind of text using a Typed String or Hot Key trigger. Type the text or paste in styled text or images.
Expand text to insert your name, address, logo, signature, whatever.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Use Text Tokens</h3>
<p>Use all sorts of expressive tokens like today's date in any format, window positions, wireless networks, Safari document URL and much more.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Clipboard History</h3>
<p>Keyboard Maestro keeps a complete history of your clipboards, so you&rsquo;ll never lose your clipboard again.  Copy three things, then paste them all into another application.</p>
</div>

<div class="clear">
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Click the Mouse</h3>
<p>Move, click, double click and drag, any button, optionally with modifiers anywhere on the screen or in a window, and more.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Manipulate Windows</h3>
<p>Resize, reposition, bring to front, close, zoom, minimize and more. Position windows exactly where you want them.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Menus and Buttons</h3>
<p>Press a button, select a menu, and now you can show a menu leaving it open for you to select the desired item.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Control iTunes</h3>
<p>Play a specific song or playlist, play, pause or stop, rewind or fast forward, set the rating or the volume.  And similarly for QuickTime Player.</p>
</div>

<div class="clear">
</div>

</div>

<!-- was bar2 -->

<div class="Entries">

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Palettes</h3>
<p>Display macros in palettes (like toolbars) so you can easily select from a number of options.  Make your own custom interfaces.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Record Macros</h3>
<p>Show Keyboard Maestro how to do something.  You can also record Quick Macros on the fly for immediate playback.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Open</h3>
<p>Open a file, folder, application, URL, system preference pane, the current Finder selection or even the CD tray.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Notifications</h3>
<p>Keyboard Maestro can show notifications, display alerts, prompt for extra information, beep, speak, or play a sound.</p>
</div>

<div class="clear">
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Execute Scripts</h3>
<p>Execute AppleScripts, or shell scripts like perl, python or ruby. Execute Automator workflows, or filter your clipboard with BBEdit Text Factories.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Built in Flow Control</h3>
<p>Use While or Until loops, For Each loops, Repeat loops, If Then Else conditionals, Pause Until and other powerful constructs.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>File Actions</h3>
<p>Move, copy, rename, trash, delete files. Read and write images and styled text in a variety of formats.</p>
</div>

<div class="Entry">
<h3>Image Actions</h3>
<p>Screen capture, find an image on the screen, flip, rotate, resize, trim, crop images and even draw shapes and text.</p>
</div>

</div>
