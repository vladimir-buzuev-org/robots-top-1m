		<p><span style="color: #0000ff; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>GSDI SDI Small Grants Program Award Winners Announced</strong></span></p>
<p>The GSDI Association’s call for proposals for the Small Grants Program 2017 stimulated 31 proposals from 27 countries in Africa, Asia-Pacific, Europe, North and South America, covering a wide range of SDI and GI/GIS related project work. Each of the award winners will receive cash support for their proposed projects of US$ 2,500.</p>
<p>Some of the proposals, including both award winners and those for whom funding was not available, also requested support from the GISCorps of URISA. These are being notified to GISCorps for any further action that they may be able to take, working within the resources of their excellent volunteer network.</p>
<p>Thanks to the generosity of GSDI’s Members and partners such as URISA GISCorps, GSDI is able to support seven of the proposals in 2017, with cash awards from GSDI’s own reserve funds. We seek further Small Grants Program Supporters so that others of the more deserving proposals can be funded. Support funding can be applied to specific projects, countries or regions.</p>

<ul>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="background-color: #ffff00;">&nbsp;<em><strong><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif; font-size: 12pt;">"The Global Spatial Data Infrastructure Association - Advancing a Location Enabled World”</span></strong></em></span></p>
<hr />
<p style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;The GSDI Association is an inclusive organization of academic and research institutions, government agencies, commercial firms, NGOs and individuals from around the world.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The purpose of the organization is to promote international cooperation and collaboration in support of local, national and international <span style="color: #000000;"><em><strong>Spatial Data Infrastructure</strong></em> </span>research, education, capacity building and implementation challenges, issues and good practice from around the globe that will allow nations to better address social, economic, and environmental issues of pressing importance.</p>
<p>Find out more <a href="/index.php/about-gsdi.html" hreflang="undefined">About GSDI</a> !</p>
<hr />
<p style="text-align: justify;"><em><strong><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Our Vision</span> is a world where everyone can readily discover, access and apply geographic information to improve their daily lives.</span></strong></em></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><em><strong><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Our Purpose</span> is to encourage international cooperation that stimulates the implementation and development of national, regional and local spatial data infrastructures.</span></strong></em></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><em><strong><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Our Mission</span> is to advance geo-information best practices, knowledge sharing and capacity building for the improved sharing and application of geographic information.</span></strong></em></p> 							</article>
