	          <h1>About the Grants </h1>     <section id="content"><div class="inner"><div class="left">
	 <p>The Rufford Foundation is a UK registered charity which funds nature conservation projects across the developing world. To date the Foundation has awarded grants to over 4000 projects in 156 countries.</p>
<p>There are <a href="/rsg/criteria">five stages of funding</a> and applications from conservationists are welcome through the <a href="https://apply.ruffordsmallgrants.org">dedicated website</a>.</p>

<div class="text full"><h4>A new species of frog has been discovered in Brazil. </h4>
