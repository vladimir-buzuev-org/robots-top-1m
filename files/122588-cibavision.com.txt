          <div class="field-item even"><h2>Alcon and CIBA Vision</h2>
<p>As the world leader in eye care, <a href="https://www.alcon.com/">Alcon</a> is committed to advancing innovation and addressing unmet patient needs.</p>
<p>
</p><div class="media media-element-container media-default media-align-center"><img height="450" width="800" class="media-element file-default" alt="" title="" src="https://www.alcon.com/sites/www.alcon.com/files/alcon-cibavision.jpg" /><br /><div class="field field-name-field-caption field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
<div class="field-items">
<div class="field-item even"></div>

</div>
</div>

<p>To further this effort, we have combined the CIBA Vision portfolio of contact lenses and lens care products with the <a href="https://www.alcon.com/eye-care-products">Alcon portfolio</a>. The two companies will now be united under the Alcon corporate brand. Coming together as one global business enables us to serve the full spectrum of eye care needs for patients around the world.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.alcon.com/our-work">Learn more</a> about Alcon and how we're dedicated to helping people see better.</p>
