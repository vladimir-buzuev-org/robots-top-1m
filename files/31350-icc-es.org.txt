<h3><a href="/index.cn.shtml"><img src="/images/cn/To-Chinese.jpg" alt="To Chinese" width="80" height="30" border="0" align="right" /></a>ICC-ES: The leader of technical<br />
evaluations of building products - Providing Peace of Mind to the Construction Industry<br />
</h3>
	<p class="splashtext">&nbsp;<br />
For decades, ICC-ES has been the industry leader in performing technical evaluations for code compliance, providing regulators and construction professionals with clear evidence that products comply with codes and standards.<br />
<br />
Having completed hundreds of evaluations of building products, components, methods, and materials, ICC-ES has a proven track record of excellence in product evaluations and customer service.<br />
<br />
Code officials trust ICC-ES because they know ICC-ES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Is compliant to  Section 1703 of the <em>International Building Code</em>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Has decades of experience in product evaluations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintains a high quality of evaluation. This provides them peace of mind knowing they have approved products that comply with the requirements of the codes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As a subsidiary of the International Code Council, uses the most up-to-date and comprehensive information about codes and standards.</p>
</li>

</ul>
