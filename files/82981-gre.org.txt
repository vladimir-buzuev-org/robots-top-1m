<p><span class="text-bold">The <span class="text-italic">GRE</span><sup class="reg">&reg;</sup> General Test gives you more opportunities for success.</span></p>
<p>Whether you are considering graduate school or business school, or still deciding what to do, you only need to take the one test accepted by thousands of graduate and business schools <a href="/s/gre/pdf/gre_aidi_fellowships.pdf"><span class="text-bold">worldwide</span></a>, the <span class="text-italic">GRE</span><sup class="reg">&reg;</sup> General Test. It is the only graduate-level admissions test that lets you skip questions and go back, change your answers and tackle the questions you want to answer first.</p>
<p>Plus, the GRE General Test provides the <a href="/gre/revised_general/about/scoreselect/?WT.ac=grehome_grescoreselect_150213"><span class="text-italic text-bold">ScoreSelect</span><sup class="reg">&reg;</sup> <span class="text-bold">option</span></a>, which means you can take a GRE test now or again in the future, and only send schools your best set of scores. Best of all, you have five years to decide how to use <span class="text-italic">any</span> of your scores.</p>
<p>Prepare with official test prep &mdash; including <a href="/gre/revised_general/prepare/?WT.ac=gre_officialguide_170601&amp;open=purchase"><span class="text-bold"><span class="text-italic">The Official Guide to the GRE</span><sup class="reg">&reg;</sup> <span class="text-italic">General Test, Third Edition</span></span></a> and <a href="/gre/revised_general/prepare/?WT.ac=gre_scoreitnow_170601&amp;open=purchase" class="text-bold"><span class="text-italic">ScoreItNow!</span><span class="tm">&trade;</span> Online Writing Practice</a> &mdash; all designed to help you do your best!</p>
<p>Don't wait. Register today, before seats are filled.</p>
