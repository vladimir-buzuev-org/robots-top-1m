						<span>Governor&#39;s Office
						</span>
					</h2>

					
						

		
			
				<h3>
					Recovery Reinvented Event 
				</h3>
			

			
				<p>Governor Doug Burgum and First Lady Kathryn Helgaas Burgum are inviting the general public, behavioral health providers, business and community leaders, and more to save the date for <a href="http://www.recoveryreinvented.com">Recovery Reinvented</a> on Sept. 26, 2017, in Bismarck.</p>
			

			

			
		

		
						

		
			
				<h3>
					State, Tribe Collaborate on Elk Season
				</h3>
			

			
				<p>Governor Burgum and Standing Rock Sioux Chairman Dave Archambault II have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) that directs state and tribal authorities on regulating an <a href="https://www.governor.nd.gov/news/state-standing-rock-sioux-tribe-collaborate-elk-season">elk hunting season within the Standing Rock Reservation</a>.</p>

				<h3>
					State Fleet Vehicle Auction
				</h3>
			

			
				<p>The Department of Transportation will be holding an <a href="https://www.dot.nd.gov/divisions/fleet/vehauction.htm">ND State Fleet vehicle auction</a> on Thursday, September 7, 2017 starting at 11:00 AM CT at 218 Airport Rd, Bismarck.</p>

					<h2>
						<span>Department of Agriculture
						</span>
					</h2>

					
						

		
			
				<h3>
					Hunger Free ND Garden Project
				</h3>
			

			
				<p>The <a href="https://www.nd.gov/ndda/marketing-information-division/local-foods/hunger-free-nd-garden-project">Department of Agriculture</a> is committed to addressing hunger issues in our state through local food initiatives. They are encouraging farmers and gardeners to donate extra fresh fruits and vegetables to food pantries, soup kitchens, and other charitable community programs. Everyone in the state is invited to become part of the project and help your neighbors in need.</p>
