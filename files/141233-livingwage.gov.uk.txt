  <h1 class="pub-c-title__text ">
    National Minimum Wage and National Living Wage rates
  </h1>
</div>

    
<div class="govuk-govspeak direction-ltr rich-govspeak disable-youtube">
  
<div class="summary">
<p>The hourly rate for the minimum wage depends on your age and whether you’re an apprentice.</p>
</div>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>This page is also available <a href="/cyfraddau-isafswm-cyflog-cenedlaethol">in Welsh (Cymraeg)</a>.</p>
</div>

<p>You must be at least:</p>

<ul>
  <li>
<a href="/know-when-you-can-leave-school">school leaving age</a> to get the National Minimum Wage</li>
  <li>aged 25 to get the National Living Wage - the minimum wage will still apply for workers aged 24 and under</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="current-rates">Current rates</h2>

<p>These rates are for the National Living Wage and the National Minimum Wage. The rates change every April.</p>

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Year</th>
      <th>25 and over</th>
      <th>21 to 24</th>
      <th>18 to 20</th>
      <th>Under 18</th>
      <th>Apprentice</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>April 2017</td>
      <td>£7.50</td>
      <td>£7.05</td>
      <td>£5.60</td>
      <td>£4.05</td>
      <td>£3.50</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

<h3 id="apprentices">Apprentices</h3>

<p><a href="/apprenticeships-guide">Apprentices</a> are entitled to the apprentice rate if they’re either:</p>

<ul>
  <li>aged under 19</li>
  <li>aged 19 or over and in the first year of their apprenticeship</li>
</ul>

<div class="example">
<p><strong>Example</strong> An apprentice aged 22 in the first year of their apprenticeship is entitled to a minimum hourly rate of £3.50</p>
</div>

<p>Apprentices are entitled to the minimum wage for their age if they both:</p>

<ul>
  <li>are aged 19 or over</li>
  <li>have completed the first year of their apprenticeship</li>
</ul>

<div class="example">
<p><strong>Example</strong> An apprentice aged 22 who has completed the first year of their apprenticeship is entitled to a minimum hourly rate of £7.05</p>
</div>
