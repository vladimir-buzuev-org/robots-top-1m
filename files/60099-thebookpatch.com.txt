				        <p class="boldHeading">What exactly is a no fee bookstore?</p>
				        <p>If your book printing cost is $2.88 and you price your book in the bookstore to sell at $12.88 the buyer will pay us the $12.88 + shipping and handling, we keep the $2.88 printing price and pay you your $10.00 profit.</p>
				        <p>Once your profits reach $40, you are paid by PayPal on the 15th of the month for all sales from the previous month. If you don’t reach $40 the payment will roll over until that amount is earned. Checks are issued quarterly and there is a $5 processing fee.</p>
				        <p>The author determines the retail price, which must be at least $0.01 above the printing cost. The author can change the book price at any time from the Book Dashboard.</p>
				        <p>Every time a book is sold from the bookstore a copy of the sale invoice (name of purchaser redacted because of pri-vacy laws) will automatically appear in the Book Dash-board to enable the author to track their sales in real time.</p>
