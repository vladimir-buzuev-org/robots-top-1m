<h2><a href="https://www.adb.org/news/events/financing-infrastructure-connectivity-economic-corridor-development">Financing Infrastructure Connectivity for Economic Corridor Development</a></h2>
<p><p>A <a href="/news/events/financing-infrastructure-connectivity-economic-corridor-development">joint workshop</a> by ADBI and Public Debt Management Office (PDMO) Thai Ministry of Finance (MOF) on 30-31 August in Bangkok tackled economic corridor development as well as possible methods to develop and finance infrastructure connectivity. Key issues included the role of infrastructure in supporting the development of economic corridors and the spillover effects of infrastructure and private sector financing in Asia. The workshop also highlighted a range of methods for financing infrastructure connectivity. ADBI Dean Naoyuki Yoshino and Executive Director Anchana Wongsawang of the Public Infrastructure Project Financing Bureau, PDMO MOF, gave keynote speeches. ADBI Deputy Dean Chul Ju Kim and Bond Market Advisor Theeraj Athanavanich of PDMO MOF Thailand delivered the opening remarks. <a href="/news/events/financing-infrastructure-connectivity-economic-corridor-development">Read more</a>.</p></p>

<li class>
<a class="img-zoom" href="https://www.adb.org/news/events/implementation-agenda-2030-financial-economic-and-political-challenges"><img src="/sites/default/files/content-media/21251-adbi-homepage-083117.jpg" alt="Key Issues in Implementing the SDGs examined"/></a>
<div>
<h2><a href="https://www.adb.org/news/events/implementation-agenda-2030-financial-economic-and-political-challenges">Key Issues in Implementing the SDGs examined</a></h2>
<p><p>A joint <a href="/news/events/implementation-agenda-2030-financial-economic-and-political-challenges">conference</a> by ADBI and the Japanese-German Center held on 25 August in Tokyo discussed the implementation of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Key issues included SDGs financing in the form of bilateral development and peer-to-peer lending, as well as the linkages between SDGs and trade such as in health and women empowerment. The event also highlighted the role of the private sector in implementing the SDGs, for example, through greener business models and the use of artificial intelligence. ADBI Dean Naoyuki Yoshino and Japanese-German Center Secretary General Friederike Bosse delivered the opening remarks. United Nations Information Centre (UNIC)'s Director Kaoru Nemoto gave the keynote address. <a href="/news/events/implementation-agenda-2030-financial-economic-and-political-challenges">Read more</a>.</p></p>
</div> </li>
<li class>
<a class="img-zoom" href="https://www.adb.org/news/events/tax-compliance-developing-digital-economy"><img src="/sites/default/files/content-media/21116-adbi-homepage-082417v2.jpg" alt="Tax Compliance in the Digital Economy"/></a>
<div>
<h2><a href="https://www.adb.org/news/events/tax-compliance-developing-digital-economy">Tax Compliance in the Digital Economy</a></h2>
<p><p>Japan National Tax Agency Deputy Commissioner Takahiro Yasui and ADBI Dean Naoyuki Yoshino emphasized the importance of understanding the implications of a digital economy on taxation systems at the workshop: <a href="/news/events/tax-compliance-developing-digital-economy">Tax Compliance in the Developing Digital Economy</a>, held on 21-24 August at ADBI. The workshop provided opportunities to analyze the challenges posed by the development of the digital economy and develop responses to ensure fair taxation and compliance. <a href="/news/events/tax-compliance-developing-digital-economy">Read more</a>.</p></p>
</div> </li>
<li class>
<a class="img-zoom" href="http://www.serconf.org/"><img src="/sites/default/files/content-media/20971-adbi-homepage-080817.jpg" alt="ADBI hosts session on Infrastructure Investment in Asia at the Singapore Economic Review Conference"/></a>
<div>
<h2><a href="http://www.serconf.org/">ADBI hosts session on Infrastructure Investment in Asia at the Singapore Economic Review Conference</a></h2>
<p><p>ADB Institute organized a plenary session on "Infrastructure Investment in Asia" at the biennial <a href="http://www.serconf.org/">Singapore Economic Review Conference</a> on 2-4 August. Presentations included ADBI Dean Naoyuki Yoshino on spillover effects, ADB Chief Economist Yasuyuki Sawada on ADB's new infrastructure forecast report, as well as others by Economic Advisor Abdul Abiad, and Principal Financial Sector Specialist Vivek Rao. ADBI Senior Consulting Economist Peter J. Morgan, and Research Fellow Bihong Huang presented in other sessions as well. <a href="http://www.serconf.org/">Read more</a>.</p></p>
