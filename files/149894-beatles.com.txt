Up for the award alongside Beyoncé's Lemonade, Yo-Yo Ma &amp; The Silk Road Ensemble's The Music of Strangers, Steve Aoki's I'll Sleep When I'm Dead and American Saturday Night: Live From The Grand Ole Opry, <em>The Beatles: Eight Days A Week - The Touring Years</em> earned the<strong> Best Music Film</strong> honour among stiff competition at Music's Biggest Night. </p>

Thank you to Jeff Jones and the team at Apple Corps for all their support and of course The Beatles for trusting us with their amazing story." </p>
