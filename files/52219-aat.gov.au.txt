      <h1 class="halfright">Welcome to the AAT</h1>

<p><span style="line-height: 1.4285;">The AAT provides a one-stop shop for the independent review of a wide range of decisions made by the Australian Government.&nbsp; We can review decisions made under Commonwealth and Norfolk Island laws.&nbsp; </span><span style="line-height: 1.4285;">We aim to make our review process accessible, fair, just, economical, informal and quick.</span></p>

        <div class="well well-green">
  <h2 class="halfright">Centrelink & Child Support</h2>
  <p>We review decisions made by officers of the Department of Human Services, including about social security payments, family assistance payments, child support, paid parental leave and student assistance payments.</p>

<p><span style="line-height: 1.4285;">Decisions which could be reviewed in the former Social Security Appeals Tribunal are now reviewed in our Social Services &amp; Child Support Division.</span></p>

<div class="cta"><a class="btn btn-primary btn-block" href="/social-services-child-support-division">Find out more</a></div>





</div>
      </div>
      <!-- Center column -->
      <div class="col-sm-4">
        <div class="well well-blue">
  <h2 class="halfright">Migration &amp; Refugee</h2>
  <p>We review decisions made by officers of the Department of Immigration and Border Protection to refuse or cancel visas.&nbsp; We can also review decisions relating to approval and cancellation of sponsorship and nomination.</p>

<p><span style="line-height: 1.4285;">Decisions which could be reviewed in the former Migration Review Tribunal and Refugee Review Tribunal are now reviewed in our Migration &amp; Refugee Division.</span></p>

<div class="cta"><a class="btn btn-primary btn-block" href="/migration-and-refugee-division">Find out more</a></div>





</div>
      </div>
      <!-- Right column -->
      <div class="col-sm-4">
        <div class="well well-pink">
  <h2 class="halfright">Other types of decisions</h2>
  <p>We review many other types of decisions made under Commonwealth laws, including decisions about Australian citizenship, Commonwealth workers&#39; compensation, freedom of information, the <a href="/applying-for-a-review/national-disability-insurance-scheme-applicants">National Disability Insurance Scheme</a>, <a href="/applying-for-a-review/types-of-matters/review-of-taxation-and-commercial-decisions">taxation</a> and veterans&#39; pensions.</p>

<p>These decisions are reviewed in one of our other Divisions.</p>
