    <h2 id='headline'>Website Development & Web Hosting with Advantage Marketing</h2>
<div class="clear"></div>

    <div class="content">
      <p><strong></strong></p>
      <h3>Providing local businesses in the Ludington area with attentive service and quality work has always been our mission.</h3> <br />
In fact, it's been our focus since even before our company had a name. In 1991, after Lynne (Jennings) Stechschulte had spent many years helping customers as a marketing consultant and in-house advertising agency, it was time to form a company and select a name. Advantage Marketing. Technically, Advantage Marketing & Publications, Inc. <br />
<br />
<h3>To this day, we remain dedicated to being YOUR Advantage.</h3> <br />
What was once a one-woman show is now an entire team of talented and dedicated people ready to help you with your marketing goals. We are ready to make running your business a little easier, because you can trust us to take care of your website development needs.<br />
<br />
<h2>Our Experience Matters</h2>Our experience as a team transcends our decades of advertising design and producing printed publications. <strong>We have also been building websites for nearly twenty years.</strong> First, our own website creations such as HamlinLake.com and VisitLudington.com, then a dozen or so websites for Hamlin Lake businesses. Soon the internet became popular and our customer base has continue to grow ever since. We take pride in serving the same customers year after year. Repeat business is, to us, proof that we provide a valuable service.<br />
<br />
<h2>Advantages of Doing Business With Advantage Marketing</h2><strong>We are more than just competent. We care.</strong> Your website hosting and development needs are going to be well-tended with our hearts and hands. We are passionate about building and maintaining your website. We are accessible and enjoy meeting with you face to face as we work with your project. We get to know you, your dreams, your goals. Think of us as part of YOUR team, because we are. We have the talent to create a beautiful, user-friendly website for you. Our graphic designers, web developers, photographers, and staff writers all work together as a team to get the job done. Best of all--when you need us, we're here! <br />
<br />
<h2>You Have Options</h2>Advantage Marketing isn't the only web developer in the area.<br />
<br />
There are larger firms within a hundred miles very capable of building a website for you. You don't even have to meet the developer face-to-face as everything needed can technically be handled via email and phone. <strong><em>But will they care about your business and your satisfaction like we will?</em></strong><br />
<br />
<div class="pic_left_half"><img src="http://amsitemag.com/images/story_images/West/735/1.jpg" alt="designer" border="0" width="250px" /></div>There are also web builders offered through companies such as GoDaddy and Wix if you want to do it yourself along with a thousand other things your business needs to have done. <strong><em>But will you really have time to learn how to build a website?</em></strong><br />
<br />
Some developers will sell you every feature available--if you let them--all for a big budget. And that's just for the initial design, not to mention all the future changes you will need. So ask yourself: <strong><em>Do you really need all the expensive bells and whistles to promote your business?</em></strong><br />
<br />
<h3>Our custom website designs are affordable, and timely.</h3>We are happy to meet with you face to face so that we all have a good understanding of the job at hand. We're local, and only a phone call or short drive away to answer any and all questions you might have about website design and development process.<br />
<br />
<h3>Our hosting service is top-notch.</h3> No worries about down-time, hacked sites, and other problems. Our server is protected and maintained by a Michigan company known for heroic support.<br />
<br />
<h3>We are SEO experts.</h3>Search engine optimization (SEO) is a critical part of the development process. We use the latest 'best practices' to ensure that your website will be readily found when people search for your business.<br />
<br />
<h2>Technically Speaking...</h2><a href="http://www.myadvantagemarketing.com/stories/internet_website_web_glossary">Keywords, meta, SEO, search submissions, domain names, hosting</a>....we're immersed in the geeky stuff day in and day out, and we've become quite adept at integrating our web expertise into some great websites. The best part about having Advantage Marketing handle your website is that you don't have to understand all the technical details because that's our job. Relax.<br />
<br />
<h3>Building a website can be an affordable and pleasant experience. Get the advantage of having Advantage Marketing on your team.</h3> <br />
<br />
<h2>Call Advantage Marketing at (231) 845-1365 for a free website development quote.</h2>
