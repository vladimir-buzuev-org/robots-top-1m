  <div class="article-body markdown">
    <p><span>9Now is only able to show live and on demand content in Australia and does not have international streaming right. If you’re outside of Australia, you won’t be able to watch 9Now until you’re back in the country. </span></p>
<p><span>If you are viewing the content in another country or have an IP address that is not an Australian IP address, you will be unable to access live tv and on demand content.</span></p>
<p><span>To continue to watch, turn off your VPN service. If you continue to get this message, let us know via the contact form. You can find your IP address<span class="apple-converted-space"> </span><a href="http://whatismyipaddress.com/">here.</a></span></p>
