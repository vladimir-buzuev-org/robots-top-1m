<section id="UseCases" class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-md-10 col-md-offset-1 text-center">
        <h2><a href="/appfog/use-cases">Use Cases</a></h2>
        <p class="subtitle text-centered">AppFog is a developer-focused Platform-as-a-Service that facilitates <strong>agile software development</strong> and removes barriers that slow innovation. The service solves a variety of fundamental problems facing developers today, and is ideal for many common enterprise workloads. Consider these four use cases for AppFog:</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="row content-margin">
      <div class="col-md-5 col-md-offset-1">
        <div class="media">
          <div class="pull-left">
            <div class="icon icon--svg">
              <img src="/assets/images/icons/app.svg" alt="AppFog lets you focus on application development, instead of hardware" title="AppFog PaaS lets you focus on strategic application development, scaling and management without being distracted by infrastructure concerns."/>
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="media-body">
            <h5 class="media-heading">Focus on Your Apps, Not Your Hardware</h5>
            <p>Enterprise data centers are bursting at the seams with key applications that were custom built for different business units. Many of these <strong>legacy applications are still business-critical</strong>, but sit on underutilized, dedicated on-premises hardware that wastes space and money. By migrating these applications to AppFog on CenturyLink Cloud, customers can take advantage of multi-tenancy and <a href="/appfog/use-cases#cost-savings">achieve significant cost savings</a> while not sacrificing the uptime or reliability of these critical applications.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="col-md-5">
        <div class="media">
          <div class="pull-left">
            <div class="icon icon--svg">
              <img src="/assets/images/icons/temporary.svg" alt="Deploy temporary applications with AppFog PaaS in seconds" title="AppFog lets you rapidly prototype solutions or quickly publish temporary or seasonal applications for increased innovation and IT agility."/>
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="media-body">
            <h5 class="media-heading">Temporary Applications</h5>
            <p>As the speed of business continues to increase, <a href="/appfog/use-cases#innovation">innovation and entrepreneurship</a> is highly valued, and the pressure is on IT to deliver software and solutions even faster. Traditional means of deploying software to dedicated servers makes it difficult to <strong>rapidly prototype solutions or quickly publish temporary or seasonal applications</strong>. By its very nature as a PaaS, applications can be deployed to AppFog in seconds. Once enabled on CenturyLink Cloud, these apps inherently have the enterprise-grade support needed for reliable, global applications that may be online for days, months or years.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="row content-margin">
      <div class="col-md-5 col-md-offset-1">
        <div class="media">
          <div class="pull-left">
            <div class="icon icon--svg">
              <img src="/assets/images/icons/agile.svg" alt="Develop and deploy Modern, Cloud-Native Applications with AppFog PaaS" title="Cloud-based Platform-as-a-Service AppFog enables agile delivery of cloud-native apps and migration of existing business applications to the cloud." />
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="media-body">
            <h5 class="media-heading">Modern, Cloud-Native Applications</h5>
            <p>In addition to migrating existing business applications to the cloud, enterprises are increasingly looking to cloud services for <strong>hosting new, modern, cloud-native applications</strong>. AppFog is a cloud-based Platform-as-a-Service that encourages <a href="/appfog/use-cases#speed-to-market">rapid delivery of cloud-native applications</a>. Specifically, AppFog PaaS gives developers their choice of programming languages to build and deploy applications on the most popular runtimes, including Go, Java and Node.js.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="col-md-5">
        <div class="media">
          <div class="pull-left">
            <div class="icon icon--svg">
              <img src="/assets/images/icons/web-apps.svg" alt="AppFog PaaS easily hosts multiple public-facing web properties" title="AppFog PaaS supports most popular web application runtimes, for ease of hosting multiple public-facing web properties." />
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="media-body">
            <h5 class="media-heading">Web Applications</h5>
            <p>Each AppFog application receives a unique URL which can be mapped to a custom domain, enabling the PaaS to host numerous public-facing web properties. Thanks to AppFog's support for the most popular web application runtimes, it can host a diverse range of public websites while providing the deep performance that enterprise IT customers demand.</p>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="row content-margin-double">
      <div class="col-md-12">
        <p class="text-center lead">Read more about <a href="/appfog/use-cases">AppFog Use Cases</a></p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
