                		<h3><a href="/about">About Canadiana</a></h3>
                		<p>Canadiana.org is a non-profit and charitable organisation founded in 1978 to preserve Canada&#39;s heritage and make it accessible for future generations. Canadiana&rsquo;s membership includes libraries, archives and universities from across Canada. We have created 40 million pages of digitized content, made available through various online portals and preserved in a certified Trustworthy Digital Repository.</p>
