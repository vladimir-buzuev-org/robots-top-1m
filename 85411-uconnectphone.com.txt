<div id="pairingTips" class="modal fade" role="dialog">
    <div class="modal-dialog">
        <div class="modal-content">
            <div class="modal-header">
                <button type="button" class="close" data-dismiss="modal">
                    <i class="icon-close"></i>
                </button>
                <h4 class="modal-title">Helpful tips to improve Bluetooth performance with your Uconnect® system</h4>
            </div>
            <div class="modal-body">
                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">Your phone won't pair to system</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Perform a hard reset in the phone by removing the battery (if removable – see your phone's owner's manual).
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            Delete the pairing history in your phone and Uconnect® system, which is usually found in the phone's Bluetooth® settings.
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            Verify you are selecting “Uconnect” in the discovered Bluetooth devices on your phone.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">Your phone won't reconnect to the system after pairing</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Set your phone to "auto-connect" or "trusted device" within the phone's Bluetooth® settings
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            Perform a factory reset on your phone. Refer to your phone manufacturer or cellular provider for instructions.
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            Many phones don't automatically reconnect after being restarted. Your phone can still be connected manually.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">Your phonebook didn't download</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Check “Do not ask again," then accept the “phonebook download” request on your phone.
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            Up to 2000 contact names with four numbers per contact will transfer to the system's phonebook.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">Text messaging through your system doesn't work</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Check “Do not ask again," then accept the “connect to your messages” request on your phone.
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            Verify that your phone has the Bluetooth® feature (Message Access Profile).
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">You can't make a conference call*</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Please keep in mind, Verizon, Sprint and US Cellular do not support conference calling.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">You can't make a call while connected to the AUX jack</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Plugging in your phone to AUX while connected to Bluetooth® will disable Hands-Free Calling. Do not make calls while your phone is plugged into the AUX jack.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="tips">
                    <p class="priority">You're having trouble streaming music via Bluetooth®</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            Before driving, start the music app on your phone. Your phone may have closed the app to conserve battery. Restart it by opening the music app and pressing play.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="disclaimer">
                    *Not available in Canada
                </div>
            </div>
