	                <div class="featurefirst"><p><IFRAME height=270 src="//www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/CShK62vCIS8" frameBorder=0 width=480 allowfullscreen></IFRAME> 
<CENTER></CENTER><BR><BR>
<P><STRONG><FONT color=#ff6600 size=3>What is the Health and Care Professions Council?<BR></FONT></STRONG>
<P>We regulate health and care professionals in the UK and were set up to protect the public. We only register professionals who meet <A title="our standards" class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/aboutregistration/standards/"><STRONG>our standards</STRONG></A> for their training&#0044; professional skills and behaviour. We can take action against health professionals who fall below these standards. We can also prosecute those who pretend to be registered.</P>
<P><STRONG><FONT color=#ff6600 size=3>What does this mean for me?<BR></FONT></STRONG>This means that professionals registered with us are genuine and meet national <A title=standards class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/aboutregistration/standards/"><STRONG>standards</STRONG></A>. This offers you protection if professionals fail to meet these standards. You can also check with us that a professional is registered.</P>
<P>You may see these health professions in an NHS clinic&#0044; privately&#0044; at home&#0044; in hospital or at school.</P>
<P><STRONG><FONT color=#ff6600 size=3>Our main functions<BR></FONT></STRONG>To protect the public&#0044; we:<BR><BR>- set <A title=standards class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/aboutregistration/standards/"><STRONG>standards</STRONG></A> for registrants&#0039; education and training&#0044; professional skills&#0044; conduct&#0044; performance and ethics;<BR><BR>- keep a <A title=register class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/check/"><STRONG>register</STRONG></A> of professionals who meet those standards;<BR><BR>- <A title="approve programmes" class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/education/programmes/"><STRONG>approve programmes</STRONG></A> which professionals must complete to register with us; and<BR><BR>- <A title="take action when professionals" class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/complaints/"><STRONG>take action when professionals</STRONG></A> on our Register do not meet our standards.<BR><BR>Each of the professions that we regulate has one or more &#0039;<A title="protected titles" class=generallink href="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/aboutregistration/protectedtitles/"><STRONG>protected titles</STRONG></A>&#0039;. Only those on the Register can legally use one of these titles.</P><BR><STRONG><FONT color=#ff6600 size=3><IMG border=0 alt="Professionals must be registrered so you can be sure they are genuine" src="http://www.hcpc-uk.org/Assets/images/10003B72public-banner.jpg"><BR>
<CENTER><BR></CENTER></FONT></STRONG></p></div>
