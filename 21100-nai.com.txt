Our complete security solutions extend beyond virus software and antimalware protection to&nbsp;<a href="/us/products/data-center-security/server-security.aspx">server security</a>,&nbsp;<a href="/us/products/siem/index.aspx">SIEM</a>, and intrusion prevention systems (IPS). Backed by McAfee Global Threat Intelligence, our solutions help companies enhance visibility into their security postures, allowing business to embrace virtualization, cloud services, and mobile devices, while protecting critical assets and sensitive data, and improving&nbsp;<a href="/us/solutions/optimize-operations/automate-incident-response.aspx">incident response</a>.</p>

                                  <h5>Our industry-leading security offerings include:</h5>

                                     <li><a href="/us/products/siem/index.aspx">McAfee SIEM</a>: Bringing together event, threat, and risk data, McAfee SIEM solutions provide real-time visibility into all security activities to improve compliance management and speed up incident response times.</li>
