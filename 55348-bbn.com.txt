<h2>Innovation on Demand</h2>

<p>Raytheon BBN Technologies is one of Raytheon&rsquo;s premier research and development centers. Our diverse research portfolio combines the best technologies to deliver innovative, custom solutions with real-world benefit. </p>


<ul>
  <li>Our world-class quantum research team is enabling next generation quantum sensing, quantum communications, and quantum computing. </li>
  <li>BBN&rsquo;s multi-sensor processing systems are in use in the U.S Navy, the UK Royal Air Force and the Canadian Navy. </li>
  <li>Our software has been a major part of every generation of North American tactical IP military network. </li>
  <li>We continue to lead in speech recognition technology, making dramatic improvements in accuracy. </li>
</ul>
