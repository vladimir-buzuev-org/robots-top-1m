	<header class="entry-header">
		<h1 class="entry-title">Welcome to Taste of Huron!</h1>
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->
    
	
	<div class="entry-content post_content">
		<div style="font-size: 13px;">
<h3 style="text-align: center;"><a href="//www.tasteofhuron.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/BlackDogTOHWinterDining1-e1424555827495.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-4896 alignleft" style="margin: 6px;" title="Braised Beef Cheeks" alt="Braised Beef Cheeks with Winter Vegetables and Parslied Egg Noodles" src="//www.tasteofhuron.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/BlackDogTOHWinterDining1-300x225.jpg" width="300" height="225" /></a></h3>
<p>Taste of Huron is an award-winning member-based program of Huron Tourism Association with a mission to celebrate Huron County’s Harvest by promoting locally produced, sustainable foods and culinary excellence on Ontario’s West Coast!</p>
<p><b>Taste of Huron takes place in Huron County, Ontario’s West Coast, located along the shores of Lake Huron. </b>This vibrant rural community is the most agriculturally productive county in Ontario, and a leader in numerous areas of agricultural technology and innovation. Huron hosts many food related events across the County and helps to promote local food and culinary initiatives.</p>
<p><strong>Taste of Huron is a Culinary program,</strong> which highlights local food, farmers, culinary experiences and more. Relax in the sun on the shores of sparkling Lake Huron, explore the picturesque countryside and heritage streetscapes, experience a breathtaking sunset and discover why <a title="Ontario's West Coast" href="http://www.ontarioswestcoast.ca/">Huron County</a> is a vacation destination of charm, culture, beauty and endless possibilities.</p>
<p><strong>Navigate your way</strong> through Huron’s small villages and towns to experience <a title="Taste of Huron" href="http://www.ontarioswestcoast.ca/owc_directory/tasteofhuron.php">local food</a> at farmer’s markets, rural festivals, road side stands and in our best restaurants in menus designed by professional, creative chefs.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
