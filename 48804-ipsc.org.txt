function slideSwitch() {
    var $active = $('#slideshow DIV.active');

    if ( $active.length == 0 ) $active = $('#slideshow DIV:last');

    // use this to pull the divs in the order they appear in the markup
    var $next =  $active.next().length ? $active.next()
        : $('#slideshow DIV:first');

    // uncomment below to pull the divs randomly
    // var $sibs  = $active.siblings();

    $active.addClass('last-active');

        .addClass('active')
        .animate({opacity: 1.0}, 1000, function() {
            $active.removeClass('active last-active');
        });
}

<h2>IPSC was established to promote, maintain,<br />
    and advance practical marksmanship.</h2>
<img src="/images/IPSC_parachuting_Serbia.jpg" width="202" height="136" alt="marksman" class="article_img_left ipsclogo" />


<div class="cushycms" id="article">
<p>The International Practical Shooting Confederation (IPSC) was established
to promote, maintain, improve and advance the sport of IPSC shooting, to
safeguard its principles and to regulate its conduct worldwide in order to
cultivate the safe, recreational use of firearms by persons of good character.</p>

<p>The art of shooting can be traced back as far as the middle ages but
it wasn&#39;t until the 19th century that shooting really developed into
a sport.</p>

<p>In the 200 years since, shooters have come together in a variety of
organizations, at the local, national, and world levels, to practice,
perfect, and perpetuate the shooting sports.</p>

<p>Competitive IPSC-style shooting developed in southern California in
the late 1950&#39;s and quickly spread throughout the shooting world
to Australia, Central and South America, Europe, and Southern Africa.</p>

<p>As the sport attracted greater interest, the participants sought a
more structured competition environment. As a result, in May of 1976,
the <a href="ipsc/columbiaconference.php">International Pistol Conference</a>
was held in Columbia, Missouri where sportsmen from around the world
participated in determining the structure, organization, and future of
IPSC marksmanship.
A <a href="ipsc/constitution.pdf" target="_blank">constitution</a>
was established and the Confederation was born.</p>

<p>Accuracy, power, and speed were recognized as the quintessential
elements and have become the foundation of IPSC shooting.
The Latin motto Diligentia, Vis, Celeritas (DVC) meaning accuracy, power,
and speed was introduced to reflect these balanced elements.
Range procedures and rules for
<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UL5Bs8auOxQ&list=UU968ZWwtGm9FYFlbHw7GJQg" target="_blank">competitions</a>,
as well as safe gun handling
standards, were also adopted.</p>

<p>At the conclusion of the Columbia conference, the International
Practical Shooting Confederation was officially formed.
Colonel Jeff Cooper, who acted as chairman of the conference,
was elected the first IPSC world president.</p>
</div>

<blockquote class="cushycms" id="blockquote"><p>IPSC shooting represents a new, exciting form of<br />
&nbsp; sport shooting and was established to promote,<br />
&nbsp; maintain, and advance practical marksmanship.</p>
</blockquote>

<div class="cushycms" id="article_continued">
<p>Under the direction of the IPSC president, the organization has
established more than ninety active
<a href="ipsc/regions.php">Regions</a> (countries) around the world
from Argentina to Zimbabwe.
Since 1975, sixteen (Level V) <a href="results/hoc.php">World Championships</a>
have been held and a much greater number of (Level IV)
<a href="results/hoc.php">Continental Championships</a>.</p>
