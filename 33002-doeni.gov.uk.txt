            <div class="pane-content">
              <div class="view view-topics view-id-topics view-display-id-block_1 view-dom-id-a93237d9673aa373e026ea7eb6173331">
                <div class="view-content">
                  <div class="views-row-odd views-row-first columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/waste">Waste</a></h3>
                    <p> Promoting a more sustainable approach to dealing with waste in Northern Ireland by&nbsp;drafting waste legislation and implementating waste management policy&nbsp; </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/waste" title="Read more in Waste">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
                  <div class="views-row-even columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/water">Water</a></h3>
                    <p> Protecting the aquatic environment by regulating water quality and hydrological processes whilst considering the needs of industry, agriculture and the protection of public health. </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/water" title="Read more in Water">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
                  <div class="views-row-odd columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/pollution">Pollution</a></h3>
                    <p> Activities undertaken to prevent, monitor and control pollution of the air, land and water </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/pollution" title="Read more in Pollution">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
                  <div class="views-row-even columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/biodiversity">Biodiversity</a></h3>
                    <p> Protecting and preserving Northern Ireland&#39;s wildlife and habitats from invasive alien species and other environmental threats. </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/biodiversity" title="Read more in Biodiversity">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
                  <div class="views-row-odd columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.communities-ni.gov.uk/topics/historic-environment">Historic environment</a></h3>
                    <p> Recording, protecting, conserving and promoting our diverse built heritage&nbsp;so that it can be enjoyed by future generations.&nbsp; </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.communities-ni.gov.uk/topics/historic-environment" title="Read more in Historic environment">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
                  <div class="views-row-even columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/land-and-landscapes">Land and landscapes</a></h3>
                    <p> Information on land quality and soil as well as details on the geology of Northern Ireland&#39;s distinctive and precious landscapes. </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/land-and-landscapes" title="Read more in Land and landscapes">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
                  <div class="views-row-odd columnItem">
                    <h3><a href="https://www.infrastructure-ni.gov.uk/topics/road-users">Road users</a></h3>
                    <p> Promoting and improving road safety via education, licensing and regulation of transportation. </p>
                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.infrastructure-ni.gov.uk/topics/road-users" title="Read more in Road users">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>

                    <span class="field-content"><a href="https://www.daera-ni.gov.uk/topics/marine" title="Read more in Marine">&hellip;&nbsp;more</a></span> </div>
