                          <h1 class="core-title">OTC Bulletin Board (OTCBB)</h1>
                      <div about="/industry/otcbb/otc-bulletin-board-otcbb" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document" role="article" class="node node--appsupportdoc node--full node--appsupportdoc--full">

  <div class="node__content">
    
<div class="field">
    <div class="field-items">
          <div class="field-item even field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden" property="content:encoded"><p>The OTC Bulletin Board (or OTCBB) is an interdealer quotation system that is used by subscribing FINRA members to reflect market making interest in OTCBB-eligible securities (as defined by <a href="http://www.finra.org/finramanual/rules/r6530/" target="">FINRA Rule 6530</a>). Subscribing market makers can utilize the OTCBB to enter, update, and display their proprietary quotations in individual securities on a real-time basis. Such quotation entries may consist of a priced bid and/or offer; an unpriced indication of interest (including "bid wanted" or "offer wanted" indications); or a bid/offer accompanied by a modifier to reflect unsolicited customer interest. Members that submit quotes in OTCBB-eligible securities to the OTCBB must comply with the Rule <a href="http://www.finra.org/finramanual/rules/r6400/" target="">6400</a> and <a href="http://www.finra.org/finramanual/rules/r6500/" target="">6500</a> Series, as well as all other applicable rules and regulations.</p>
