<section id="intro">
<p>The Melbourne Institute is Australia&rsquo;s pre-eminent economic and social policy research institution. It undertakes high quality, independent and impartial applied research, and contributes to the development of public policy in Australia.</p><p>Established in 1962, the Institute provides high calibre, peer-reviewed levels of research to government, business and community groups. It is internationally renowned for its HILDA and MABEL Surveys, as well as its measurement of economic and social indicators.</p><p><a href="http://melbourneinstitute.unimelb.edu.au/about" class="button-hero with-arrow">Find out more about us</a></p>
