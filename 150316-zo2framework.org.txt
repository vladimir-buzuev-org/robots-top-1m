		}).mouseleave(function() {

		});
	});

<h1 class="itemTitle zt-heading">ZO2 - Flexible and Responsive Joomla Templates Framework</h1>
<p>ZO2 Framework is a free, open-source, highly extensible, search-engine optimized Joomla Templates Framework featuring responsive web design, bootstrap framework, styling for popular extensions, and a whole community behind it. ZO2 includes a ton of features for both developers and designers, and can be customized even without coding knowledge.</p>
