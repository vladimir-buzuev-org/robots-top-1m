<h1>tape.tv 2.0 is coming soon</h1>
<h2>we are going mobile</h2>
<p>Eight years ago the first and largest music video platform, <a href="http://www.tape.tv">tape.tv</a> was founded in Europe, which was so far unique in the music industry. The most creative and committed minds of the media industry have done their best to lead the fight for music. There were many memorable moments, one of the most notable being the "On the Roofs" concert with Florence and the Machine, with the young and unknown <a href=".edsheeran.com">Ed Sheeran</a> or even with Cro.
<p>The development of mobile technologies and online viedo companies changed the landscape of the music video industry (the revolution leaded by <a href="https://youtube.com">YouTube</a>) and the future of the original tape.tv became uncertain.
<p>Tape.tv is now owned by Appolicious inc., a recognized expert in mobile app technology both on <a href="https://androidapps.com">Android</a> and <a href="https://appolicious.com">iPhone</a>. We believe that the original idea can be rebuilt on mobile. We are in talk with music industry experts and started developing groundbreaking mobile apps on tape.tv. We know that it is an extremely competitive field with excellent apps, like <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shazam.android">Shazam</a>. Still, we believe that the mix of the original concept of tape.tv and mobile technology can be combined into a unique and great service.
<p style="text-align:left; padding-left:50px;">What to expect from tape.tv 2.0:<br>
- free and original music videos<br>
- fresh talents<br>
- community content<br>
- music recognition technology<br>
- offline mode<br>
- Android and iOS version<br>
and many more...
<p>Music is what connects us all. This has always been the case and will always remain so. We are looking forward to the future with great joy and great expectations and are curious about what we can still achieve together. Please stay tuned!
