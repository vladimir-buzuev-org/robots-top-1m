<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>21 August 2017 -- NEWS RELEASE</strong></span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>Aquisition in the USA</strong></span></p>

<p>ORAFOL Europe GmbH is pleased to announce the acquisition of Rowland Technologies, Inc of Wallingford, CT. Rowland Technologies is a specialty manufacturer of high-quality plastic films and sheeting. Their unique extrusion process and proprietary in-house tooling techniques allows Rowland to make progressive solutions for a wide range of applications worldwide.<br> <br> Rowland’s diverse portfolio of high-performance products, include RowTec® polycarbonate film, SolaTuf® impact-modified acrylic film, and Rowlux® illusion film that deliver the consistency and reliability demanded by today's' exacting standards. From graphic arts materials to medical packaging, and from aerospace applications to automotive parts, Rowland Technologies films give customers the quality they desire.<br> <br> The latest addition to the ORAFOL Group enhances their production capabilities and product offerings while ensuring quality standards for a key raw material.<br> “On behalf of Rowland Technologies Inc., we are extremely pleased and excited to be a part of the ORAFOL Team. I am confident that Rowland’s unique capabilities and products will complement ORAFOL’s vast global presence. We are looking forward to integrating into the ORAFOL family and sharing our mutual strengths that will benefit our Customers” said Stephen DiMugno, President/CEO, Rowland Technologies.<br> <br> “For a number of years Rowland Technologies has been a key supplier to ORAFOL and has shared our commitment to a long-term strategic view focused on the highest standards of quality and innovation where dynamic growth, innovative products, and increased customer satisfaction are assured” said Dr. Holger Loclair, Managing Director, ORAFOL Europe GmbH.<br> <br> The Connecticut based company will continue operations in their newly expanded 70,000 square foot facility under the name Rowland Technologies, An ORAFOL Company.</p>
