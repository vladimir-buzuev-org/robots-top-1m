function increaseFontSize() {
   var p = document.getElementsByTagName('div');
   for(i=0;i<p.length;i++) {
      if(p[i].style.fontSize) {
         var s = parseInt(p[i].style.fontSize.replace("px",""));
      } else {
         var s = 12;
      }
      if(s!=maxpx) {
         s += 1;
      }
      p[i].style.fontSize = s+"px"
   }
};

function decreaseFontSize() {
   var p = document.getElementsByTagName('div');
   for(i=0;i<p.length;i++) {
      if(p[i].style.fontSize) {
         var s = parseInt(p[i].style.fontSize.replace("px",""));
      } else {
         var s = 12;
      }
      if(s!=minpx) {
         s -= 1;
      }
      p[i].style.fontSize = s+"px"
   }   
};

function restoreFontSize() {
   var p = document.getElementsByTagName('div');
   for(i=0;i<p.length;i++) {
      if(p[i].style.fontSize) {
         var s = parseInt(p[i].style.fontSize.replace("px",""));
      } else {
         var s = restore;
      }
      if(s!=minpx) {
         s -= 1;
      }
      p[i].style.fontSize = restore + "px"
   }   
};

<div class="ttlred" id="ttlpos">Overview</div></div>The Office of Housing provides vital public services through its nationally administered programs. It oversees the <a href="HUD/program_offices/housing/fhahistory">Federal Housing Administration (FHA)</a> the largest mortgage insurer in the world, as well as regulates housing industry business.<br />
<br />
The mission of the Office of Housing is to:<br />
<br />
<img alt=" - " height="10" src="images/hudimg?id=redbullet_40265.bmp" width="10" /> Contribute to building and preserving healthy neighborhoods and communities<br />
<img alt=" - " height="10" src="images/hudimg?id=redbullet_40265.bmp" width="10" /> Maintain and expand homeownership, rental housing and healthcare opportunities<br />
<img alt=" - " height="10" src="images/hudimg?id=redbullet_40265.bmp" width="10" /> Stabilize credit markets in times of economic disruption<br />
<img alt=" - " height="10" src="images/hudimg?id=redbullet_40265.bmp" width="10" /> Operate with a high degree of public and fiscal accountability<br />
<img alt=" - " height="10" src="images/hudimg?id=redbullet_40265.bmp" width="10" /> Recognize and value its customers, staff, constituents and partners<br />
<br />
<div id="about" style="display: none">
<div>Within the Office of Housing are five business areas:</div><br />
<div class="genportletlink"><a href="http://portal.hud.gov/hudportal/HUD?src=/program_offices/housing/sfh">Single Family Housing</a></div>
<div>HUD&#39;s Single Family programs include mortgage insurance on loans to purchase new or existing homes, condominiums, manufactured housing, houses needing rehabilitation, and for reverse equity mortgages to elderly homeowners.</div><br />
<div class="genportletlink"><a href="http://portal.hud.gov/hudportal/HUD?src=/program_offices/housing/mfh">Multifamily Housing</a></div>
<div>HUD&#39;s Multifamily programs provide mortgage insurance to HUD-approved lenders to facilitate the construction, substantial rehabilitation, purchase and refinancing of multifamily housing projects.<br />
<br />
<div class="genportletlink"><a href="http://portal.hud.gov/hudportal/HUD?src=/federal_housing_administration/healthcare_facilities">Healthcare Programs</a></div>
<div>HUD&#39;s Healthcare programs provide mortgage insurance on loans that&nbsp;finance the construction, renovation, acquisition, or refinancing of healthcare facilities such as hospitals and residential care facilities.</div><br />
<div class="genportletlink"><a href="http://portal.hud.gov/hudportal/HUD?src=/program_offices/housing/sfh/hcc">Office of Housing Counseling</a></div>
<div>HUD provides support to a nationwide network of Housing Counseling Agencies (HCA) and counselors. HCA&#39;s are trained and approved to provide tools to current and prospective homeowners, renters so that they can make responsible choices to address their housing needs in light of their financial situations.</div><br />
<div class="genportletlink"><a href="http://www.hud.gov/offices/hsg/ramh/reguprog.cfm">Regulatory Programs</a></div></div>
<div>HUD&#39;s Regulatory programs are designed to assist homeowners, homebuyers, and regulate real estate transactions.</div></div>
