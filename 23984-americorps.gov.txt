AmeriCorps places thousands of young adults into intensive service positions where they learn valuable work skills, earn money for education, and develop an appreciation for citizenship.</p><p></p><div class="ac_landing_boxes"><div><h2>AmeriCorps<br />NCCC</h2><p><a href="/programs/americorps/americorps-nccc" onclick="ga('send','event','AC-Landing: NCCC','/programs/americorps/americorps-nccc')"><img alt="AmeriCorps NCCC logo" src="/sites/default/files/upload/acnccc_0.png" style="width: 150px; height: 150px; " /></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">AmeriCorps NCCC members travel the country strengthening communities and developing leadership skills on diverse teams. Members serve full-time for 10 months to address community needs and respond to disasters. AmeriCorps NCCC is comprised of two distinct service tracks: <a href="/programs/americorps/americorps-nccc">Traditional Corps</a> and <a href="/programs/americorps/fema-corps">FEMA Corps</a>.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a class="button" href="/programs/americorps/americorps-nccc" onclick="ga('send','event','AC-Landing: NCCC','/programs/americorps/americorps-nccc')">Learn More</a></p></div><div><h2>AmeriCorps<br />Network</h2><p><a href="/programs/americorps/americorps-state-and-national" onclick="ga('send','event','AC-Landing: SN','/programs/americorps/americorps-state-and-national')"><img alt="AmeriCorps Logo" src="/sites/default/files/upload/ac.png" style="width: 150px; height: 150px; " /></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">AmeriCorps Network of programs (AmeriCorps State and National) supports a wide range of local service programs that engage thousands of Americans in intensive community service each year. We provide grants to a network of local and national organizations and agencies committed to using national service to address critical community needs in education, public safety, health, and the environment. Each of these organizations and agencies, in turn, uses their AmeriCorps funding to recruit, place, and supervise AmeriCorps members nationwide.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a class="button" href="/programs/americorps/americorps-programs/americorps-network">Learn More</a></p></div><div><h2>AmeriCorps<br />VISTA</h2><p><a href="/programs/americorps/americorps-vista" onclick="ga('send','event','AC-Landing: VISTA','/programs/americorps/americorps-vista')"><img alt="AmeriCorps VISTA logo" src="/sites/default/files/upload/acvista_0.png" style="width: 150px; height: 150px; " /></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">AmeriCorps VISTA members are passionate and committed to their mission to bring individuals and communities out of poverty. Members make a year-long, full-time commitment to serve on a specific project at a nonprofit organization or public agency. They focus their efforts to build the organizational, administrative, and financial capacity of organizations that fight illiteracy, improve health services, foster economic development, and otherwise assist low-income communities.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a class="button" href="/programs/americorps/americorps-vista" onclick="ga('send','event','AC-Landing: VISTA','/programs/americorps/americorps-vista')">Learn More</a></p></div></div><p></p><!-- GovDelivery Subscription Overlay --><script src="https://content.govdelivery.com/overlay/js/3573.js"></script><!-- End GovDelivery Overlay --></div></div></div>  </div>
