	<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
				<p>Alf Clausen, the composer for almost all of the episodes of <a href="https://forum.chorus.fm/threads/the-simpsons-fox.365/"><em>The Simpsons</em></a>, has been fired. <a href="http://variety.com/2017/tv/news/simpsons-composer-alf-clausen-fired-1202543183/">Variety</a> reports:</p>
<blockquote><p>
  Two-time Emmy winner Alf Clausen has been fired from “The Simpsons” after 27 years of providing music for Bart, Lisa, and company.</p>
<p>  Clausen told Variety that he received a call from “Simpsons” producer Richard Sakai that the company was seeking “a different kind of music” and that he would no longer be scoring the longtime Fox hit.
</p></blockquote>
