          <div class="overview">

</p>
              <p class="parent_organisations">
                <abbr title="Maritime and Coastguard Agency">MCA</abbr> is an executive agency, sponsored by the <a href="/government/organisations/department-for-transport">Department for Transport</a>.
              </p>
