<h2>Skills for Care&nbsp;helps create a better-led, more skilled and valued adult social care workforce.&nbsp;</h2>
<p>We&nbsp;provide practical tools and support to help adult social care organisations and <strong><a title="Employing your own care and support" class="sys_0 sys_t10894" href="/Recruitment-retention/Employing-your-own-care-and-support/Employing-your-own-care-and-support.aspx">individual employers</a></strong> in England<strong> <a title="Recruitment and retention" class="sys_0 sys_t10894" href="/Recruitment-retention/Recruitment-and-retention.aspx">recruit</a></strong>, <strong><a title="Learning and development" class="sys_0 sys_t10894" href="/Learning-development/Learning-and-development.aspx">develop</a> </strong>and <strong><a title="Leadership and management" class="sys_0 sys_t10894" href="/Leadership-management/Leadership-and-management.aspx">lead</a></strong> their workforce.&nbsp;<strong><a title="In your area" class="sys_0 sys_t10894" href="/Getting-involved/In-your-area/In-your-area.aspx">Our experts</a></strong> are available now to provide bespoke support to your organisation.</p>
