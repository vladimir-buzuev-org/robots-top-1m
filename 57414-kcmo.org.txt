							<h3 class="custom_heading one">City offices closed on Labor Day</h3>
							<p>In observance of Labor Day, trash and recycling collection will adopt the <a href="http://kcmo.gov/neighborhoods/trash/holiday-schedule/">holiday schedule</a> next week, and City offices will be closed Monday, Sept. 4. Residents who usually have Monday trash collection will receive this service Tuesday, Sept. 5. Residents who usually have Friday trash collection will receive this service Saturday, Sept. 9.</p>
