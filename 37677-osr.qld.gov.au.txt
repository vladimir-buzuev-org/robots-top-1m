					<div     class="vc_row wpb_row section vc_row-fluid " style=' text-align:left;'><div class=" full_section_inner clearfix"><div class="wpb_column vc_column_container vc_col-sm-12"><div class="vc_column-inner "><div class="wpb_wrapper">
	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p>The Office of State Revenue (OSR) is part of Queensland Treasury. We are responsible for collecting state taxes and royalties, and administering the Queensland First Home Owners’ Grant.</p>
<p>The revenue we collect provides about $13 billion in income for Queensland. We put this money back into roads, schools, hospitals and health services, as well as art galleries and community events. This helps to grow our economy and improve the wellbeing of all Queenslanders.</p>
<p>Watch our short video to find out more about who we are and what we do.</p>

			<h2>OSRconnect</h2>
<p>Our online lodgement system, <a href="https://www.business.qld.gov.au/running-business/support-assistance/osrconnect">OSRconnect</a>, allows registered self assessors of duties, payroll tax and royalties to conveniently lodge their transactions or returns.</p>

		</div> 
	</div> 
	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<h2>Rulings</h2>
<p><a href="/taxes-and-royalties/public-rulings/">Public rulings and royalty rulings</a> set out the Commissioner’s interpretation of the laws that we administer.</p>
<p>The rulings clarify how potentially ambiguous provisions will be applied, or outline circumstances where the Commissioner can exercise discretion to waive certain requirements.</p>
<p>Rulings may also list which factors the Commissioner will consider when making a decision, or the evidence required to establish a fact.</p>

<h2>Duties</h2>
<p>We administer different types of duty under the <a href="https://www.legislation.qld.gov.au/LEGISLTN/CURRENT/D/DutiesA01.pdf">Duties Act 2001</a>. If you are a <a href="https://www.business.qld.gov.au/industry/professional-financial/transfer-duty/self-assessors">transfer duty self assessor</a>, use our <a href="https://www.business.qld.gov.au/industry/professional-financial/transfer-duty/self-assessors/toolkits">toolkits</a> to help you lodge transactions using OSRconnect.</p>
